Justin "The Executioner" Levens, and Wife Found Dead

By Matt Scott (December 23, 2008)
Posted in MMA Blog

Justin Levens (28), a moderately successful UFC fighter, is suspected of murder/suicide involving his wife Sarah(25). Sarah’s mother discovered the bodies lying in their Laguna Niguel, CA condo and notified the police at around 2:30pm this last Sunday. Investigators believe that Justin shot his wife and then himself in a joint and consented suicide. The suspected reasons are still unclear and no further information has been released.
