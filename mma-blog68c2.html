Anderson Silva Training with Freddie Roach

By Matt Scott (November 18, 2008)
Posted in MMA Blog

The news has leaked out that Anderson Silva, UFC Middleweight Champion, has begun training his boxing with world renowned trainer Freddie Roach.

Roach has been training former UFC Heavyweight Champion Andrei Arlovski for some time now, and it’s intriguing to see Roach accept more MMA fighters.

This could create even more danger for Silva’s opponents on the feet, but could also be forewarning that Silva’s career could likely be cut short due to a desire to persue professional boxing.
