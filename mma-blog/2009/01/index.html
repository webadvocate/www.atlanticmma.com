Helio Gracie Dead at 95

By Roman Skaskiw (January 29, 2009)
Posted in MMA Blog

Helio is widely credited as the founder of Gracie Jiu Jitsu, or Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.

Sherdog reports:Helio Gracie, the father of Gracie jiu-jitsu, is dead at the age of 95. Gracie passed in his sleep early Thursday in Itaipaiva, Rio de Janeiro, after he had been admitted to a local hospital a few days prior for stomach problems.
